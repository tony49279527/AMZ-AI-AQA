### 1.2 产品使用场景 (Product Usage Scenarios)

Listed from broadest to most specific applications:

从最广泛到最具体的应用场景排序:

#### **1. Metal Surface Restoration & Polishing 金属表面修复与抛光**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Restoring oxidized, tarnished, or scratched metal surfaces to original luster
- **Materials 材料:** Aluminum, stainless steel, brass, copper, bronze, cast iron
- **Use Cases 使用案例:** 
  - Automotive parts (wheels, trim, exhaust pipes) 汽车部件(轮毂、装饰条、排气管)
  - Antique restoration (fire extinguishers, tools, hardware) 古董修复(灭火器、工具、五金件)
  - Industrial equipment maintenance 工业设备维护

#### **2. Jewelry & Precious Metal Finishing 珠宝与贵金属精加工**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Achieving mirror-finish on fine jewelry and precious metals
- **Materials 材料:** Gold, silver, platinum, gemstone settings
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Ring polishing 戒指抛光
  - Bracelet restoration 手镯修复
  - Custom jewelry fabrication 定制珠宝制作

#### **3. Woodworking & Furniture Polishing 木工与家具抛光**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Buffing wood surfaces, wax application, final finishing
- **Materials 材料:** Hardwoods, finished furniture, wooden crafts
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Furniture restoration 家具修复
  - Wooden handle polishing 木质手柄抛光
  - Decorative wood finishing 装饰木材精加工

#### **4. Plastic & Composite Material Buffing 塑料与复合材料抛光**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Removing scratches, restoring clarity, surface smoothing
- **Materials 材料:** Acrylic, polycarbonate, ABS plastics
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Headlight restoration 车灯修复
  - Plastic trim polishing 塑料装饰条抛光
  - Clear plastic scratch removal 透明塑料划痕去除

#### **5. Stone & Ceramic Polishing 石材与陶瓷抛光**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Lapidary work, tile finishing, decorative stone polishing
- **Materials 材料:** Marble, granite, ceramic tiles, decorative stones
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Countertop edge finishing 台面边缘精加工
  - Decorative stone projects 装饰石材项目
  - Ceramic restoration 陶瓷修复

#### **6. Knife & Tool Sharpening/Polishing 刀具与工具磨锐/抛光**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Final polishing stage after sharpening, edge refinement
- **Materials 材料:** Steel blades, tool edges
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Kitchen knife polishing 厨刀抛光
  - Woodworking chisel finishing 木工凿精加工
  - Cutting tool maintenance 切削工具维护

#### **7. Glass & Mirror Surface Treatment 玻璃与镜面处理**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Fine polishing of glass edges, mirror surface restoration
- **Materials 材料:** Glass, mirrors, optical surfaces
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Glass edge smoothing 玻璃边缘打磨
  - Mirror defect removal 镜面瑕疵去除
  - Optical component finishing 光学元件精加工

#### **8. Small Parts & Detail Work 小型零件与细节工作**
- **Scenario Description 场景描述:** Precision polishing of small components, hard-to-reach areas
- **Materials 材料:** Small metal parts, intricate jewelry, detailed hardware
- **Use Cases 使用案例:**
  - Watch component polishing 手表零件抛光

### 1.3 适用人群、作用对象与应用范围 (Target Users, Objects & Application Scope)

**Target Users 适用人群:**
- Professional polishers and metal fabricators 专业抛光工与金属制造工
- Automotive detailers and mechanics 汽车美容师与机械师
- Jewelry makers and lapidary artists 珠宝制造商与宝石艺术家
- Woodworkers and furniture restorers 木工与家具修复师
- DIY enthusiasts and home workshop owners DIY爱好者与家庭工作坊主
- Antique collectors and restorers 古董收藏家与修复师

**Required Tools 所需工具:**
- Bench grinder (6", 8", or 10" models) 台式砂轮机(6英寸、8英寸或10英寸型号)
- Tapered spindle adapters (for 5/8" arbor conversion) 锥形主轴适配器(用于5/8英寸轴孔转换)
- Safety equipment (goggles, dust mask, gloves) 安全设备(护目镜、防尘口罩、手套)
- Buffing compounds (brown/coarse, white/medium, green/fine) 抛光剂(棕色/粗、白色/中、绿色/细)

**Materials Applicable 适用材料:**
- **Metals 金属:** Aluminum, stainless steel, brass, copper, bronze, cast iron, chrome
- **Wood 木材:** Hardwoods, finished furniture surfaces
- **Plastics 塑料:** Acrylic, polycarbonate, ABS
- **Stone 石材:** Marble, granite, decorative stones
- **Glass 玻璃:** Edges, mirrors, optical surfaces

**Skill Requirements 技能要求:**
- **Beginner Level 初级:** Understand basic bench grinder operation, safety protocols
- **Intermediate Level 中级:** Compound application techniques, pressure control, wheel selection
- **Advanced Level 高级:** Custom compound mixing, multi-stage polishing, specialized material handling

**Occasions & Settings 场合与环境:**
- Home workshops 家庭工作坊
- Professional fabrication shops 专业制造车间
- Automotive repair facilities 汽车维修设施
- Jewelry studios 珠宝工作室
- Restoration workshops 修复工作坊

1. **Impulse Purchase / Cart Abandonment 冲动购买/购物车遗留**
   - "Forgot it was in cart"
   - "Checked out of cart accidentally"
   - "Changed my mind"

2. **Duplicate Orders 重复订单**
   - "Product was double ordered by mistake"
   - "Received another package with similar items"
   - "I over ordered"

3. **Project Cancellation 项目取消**
   - "No longer needed"
   - "Sold item I was going to use it on"
   - "My needs changed"

**Analysis 分析:**
This category represents "buyer's remorse" and cart management issues rather than product defects. While partially unavoidable, clearer value proposition and use-case education can reduce this.

此类别代表"买家后悔"和购物车管理问题,而非产品缺陷。虽然部分不可避免,但更清晰的价值主张和用例教育可以减少这种情况。

#### **3. DEFECTIVE (10.6%) - 有缺陷**

**Specific Defects Reported 报告的具体缺陷:**

| **Defect Type<br>缺陷类型** | **Customer Comments<br>客户评论** | **Frequency<br>频率** |
|---|---|---|
| **Wheels Falling Apart<br>轮盘散架** | "Wheels started falling apart and shedding all over"<br>"轮盘开始散架并到处脱落" | Medium<br>中频 |
| **Does Not Fit Properly<br>不适配** | "Did not work an buffing"<br>"Did not fit buffer"<br>"Doesn't fit bench grinder"<br>"抛光不起作用"<br>"不适配抛光机"<br>"不适配台式砂轮机" | High<br>高频 |
| **Poor Quality Materials<br>材料质量差** | "Poor quality"<br>"The quality description" (sarcastic)<br>"质量差"<br>"质量描述"(讽刺) | Low<br>低频 |

**Analysis 分析:**
Most "defective" returns are actually compatibility issues (wrong size/arbor) rather than manufacturing defects. True quality defects (wheels falling apart) are relatively rare, indicating good manufacturing standards but poor customer guidance.

大多数"有缺陷"退货实际上是兼容性问题(错误尺寸/轴孔)而非制造缺陷。真正的质量缺陷(轮盘散架)相对罕见,表明制造标准良好但客户指导不足。
